How can we better manage our mood together?

We want to understand how people over the world feel, why their moods vary over time, and what can be done to make people happier. We at EgoCenter (nonprofit) are proposing a new approach to allow participants to monitor their health in real time as well as partner in research studies.

The “Moody” app will use surveys and phone sensor data to collect and track five common problems: fatigue, cognitive difficulties, sleep disturbances, mood changes and reduction in exercise performance. Some participants will also be invited to keep a health diary.
